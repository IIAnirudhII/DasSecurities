Common Questions

You asked.
We answer plainly.

Your money is invested directly with AMFI-registered mutual fund houses — not held by us. Das Securities is an advisory, not a custodian. We never touch your capital. All investments are held in your name, in your demat or folio, under SEBI-regulated fund houses. We are registered with AMFI, which means our conduct is governed by a national regulatory body.
Most mutual funds in India allow SIPs starting at ₹500 per month. For lumpsum investments, the minimum is typically ₹1,000. There is no minimum to sign up with us — we'll work with whatever you can commit, and help you scale as your income grows. Starting small and staying consistent beats waiting to invest a large sum.
Groww and Zerodha are execution platforms — they let you invest, but they don't tell you what to invest in or why. You're on your own to research, pick, and manage. Das Securities is an advisory. We do the research, build the recommendation, explain the reasoning, and stay involved as your situation evolves. Think of them as a brokerage; think of us as your financial advisor.
We operate on a commission model — we receive a small trail commission from the fund house when you invest through us. This means our advisory is at no direct cost to you. We will always be transparent about how we earn and ensure our recommendations are never influenced by commission rates — your goal comes first.
Nothing. Our advisory service is completely free for you. There are no sign-up fees, no subscription charges, and no hidden costs at any point. You pay exactly ₹0 for the advice, the portfolio recommendation, and the ongoing support. We earn through a small trail commission paid by the fund house — not by you.
Within 12 hours. One of our advisors will reach out via phone or WhatsApp — whichever you prefer — to understand your goals in detail before we make any recommendation. No automated emails, no generic PDFs. A real conversation with a real person.
